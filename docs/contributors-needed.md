# Contributors Needed

We are looking for passionate individuals to join our mission of empowering Nepali youth. We need contributors in a variety of roles:

## Roles We Need

- **Web Developers** - Help build and maintain our platform
- **Designers** - Create engaging visual content and improve user experience
- **Website Content Creators** - Develop meaningful content for our pages

## Important Information

All content on this website will remain in the **public domain** and is meant to drive the interests of Nepalese GenZ and the Youth.

To learn more about our organization, visit our [about page](/about). You can also read about our mission and goals on our [mission page](/mission).

Ready to contribute? Check out our contribution guidelines and get started!
