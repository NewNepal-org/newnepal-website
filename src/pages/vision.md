# NewNepal.org Vision

**A Digital Platform for Nepali Youth, by Nepali Youth.**

## Mission

To create a platform that:

- Connects Nepali youth across geographical, ethnic/tribal, ideological, religious, political and other divisions
- Provides a safe space for all Nepali youth identities to unite and participate in critical dialogue pertaining to Nepal's future
- Promotes youth agenda, political participation, awareness, and civil discourse

## Membership

Only people of Nepali origin can participate as members. Diaspora welcome.

## Funding

- Funding strictly for platform-related expenses, including website and platform hosting, and subscription fees
- Funding possibly only by persons of Nepali origin, home and abroad, capped at a fixed upper limit of **NRs. 10,000** (or equivalent) per month
- Financial activities including donations above **NRs. 5,000** (or equivalent) will be transparently disclosed every month

## Operationalization

- Platform events and activities will be treated as public events and may be broadcasted on social media
- Code repositories, and other non-personal assets will be made public
- Activities and processes will be feedback-driven

## Initial Activities

### Core Programs

- **Debates on Critical Issues**: We will aim to organize regular debates on critical issues to allow expression of opposing views with hope that a consensus can be formed
- **GenZ and Youth Groups Registry**: We will establish a registry of all the GenZ groups, their mission and scope, and contact details with a hope that this will facilitate coordination across groups
- **Corruption Database**: To centralize evidence and hold accountability
- **Youth News Portal**: To promote key topics related to nation-building and prevent the spreading of misinformation
- **Polling**: To understand perspectives, identify missing voices in public discourse, and inform youths

### Administrative

- Recruiting youth for participating in the platform
- Platform Development (web development, assets, etc.)
- Planning the future of the platform
