# About NewNepal.org

NewNepal.org is a Nepali youth organization dedicated to creating a public platform built by Nepali youths, for all Nepali youths. Our mission is to connect Nepali youth across geographical, ethnic, ideological, religious, and political divisions while providing a safe space for critical dialogue about Nepal's future.

## Founder

**Damodar Dahal** is the founder of NewNepal.org. He comes from Sablakhu, Taplejung, Nepal, and is a Global South scholar pursuing a Masters of Liberal Arts (ALM) degree in International Relations from Harvard University Extension School. He currently works as a Software Development Engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS).

## Our Platform

NewNepal.org serves as a hub for Nepali youth engagement, featuring debates on critical issues, youth group registries, corruption databases, news portals, and polling to understand youth perspectives on nation-building topics.
